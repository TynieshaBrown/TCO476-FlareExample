Rosemary & Thyme

A modern, upscale American restaurant that uses the freshest ingredients from the current season. Rosemary & Thyme Restaurant is a modern day example of Victorian elegance. With original tile floors , furnishings are period reproductions, including the wrought-iron footed tables, reflecting the style of the Victorian period. The beauty and splendor of Rosemary & Thyme Restaurant has held many weddings and galas and it continues to be the site of many memorable events today."

 

The Station

"Located in beautiful, downtown Americus, The Station offers great options for lunch and dinner. Anything from soups, salads, sandwiches, appetizers, chicken, seafood, and steak can be found. The Station is also equipped with a full bar!"

 

Gladys Kitchen

"Homestyle, southern cooking. Owned by a local family in Americus, Ga. Been open for over 15 years. Fried chicken, collar greens, fried green tomatoes, mac and cheese, and black berry cobbler to name a few of their specialties."

 

Floyds Pub

"Floyd's Pub was opened in 1991 after a $5.8 million restoration project that restored The BEST WESTERN PLUS Windsor Hotel to her former beauty. The pub is named after former employee Floyd Lowery, who passed away long before the hotel reopened its doors in 1991. He was a bellman and elevator operator for The Windsor Hotel. As ironic as it sounds, Floyd Lowery never had a drink in his life!"
