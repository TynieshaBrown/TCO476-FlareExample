Food

Rosemary & Thyme

A modern, upscale American restaurant that uses the freshest ingredients from the current season. Rosemary & Thyme Restaurant is a modern day example of Victorian elegance. With original tile floors, reproduced furnishings of the period it reflects the style of the Victorian period. Many people have weddings here as well!

 

The Station

Also located in downtown Americus, The Station serves lunch and dinner. Anything from soups, salads, sandwiches, appetizers, chicken, seafood, and steak can be found. The Station also has a full bar!

 

Gladys Kitchen

Homestyle, southern cooking restaurant owned by a local family in Americus, Ga. It has been open for over 15 years. They serve fried chicken, collar greens, fried green tomatoes, mac and cheese, and black berry cobbler to name a few of their specialties."
